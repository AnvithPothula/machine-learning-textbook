About Activation Functions

Sigmoid and Tanh saturate at extremes, causing vanishing gradients. The highlighted yellow regions show saturation zones where gradients approach zero.

ReLU is fast and avoids vanishing gradients but can have "dying neurons" (outputs stuck at zero). Leaky ReLU fixes this with a small negative slope (Î±).
