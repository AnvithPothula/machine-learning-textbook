About Categorical Encoding

Label Encoding assigns integer labels to categories (e.g., red=0, green=1, blue=2). It's memory-efficient but introduces artificial ordering.

One-Hot Encoding creates binary columns for each category. It avoids artificial ordering but increases dimensionality.

drop_first=True removes one column per categorical variable to avoid multicollinearity (the dummy variable trap).
