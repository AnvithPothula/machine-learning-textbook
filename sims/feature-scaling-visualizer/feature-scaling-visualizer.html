About Feature Scaling

Min-Max Scaling transforms features to the [0, 1] range. It's sensitive to outliers but preserves the original distribution shape.

Z-Score Standardization transforms features to have mean=0 and std=1. It's less sensitive to outliers and assumes Gaussian distribution.
